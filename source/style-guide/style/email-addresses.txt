.. _email-addresses:

===============
Email Addresses
===============

If you need to show an example email address, use **example.com** or
**example.org** as the domain. The Internet Assigned Numbers Authority
(IANA) reserves these domains for use in examples.

If you need to show an actual email address, use a `standalone hyperlink
<https://docutils.sourceforge.io/docs/ref/rst/restructuredtext.html#standalone-hyperlinks>`_
to make the email address live.
